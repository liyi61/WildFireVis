Abstract

Wildfires lead to damage debris, contamination to land or water, and bad air quality. Even though wildfires can cause a series of such bad consequences, there is still no enough attentions towards it. The data set I will be using is about wildfires that occurred in the Untied States from 1992 to 2015. It has data on nearly 2 million wildfires over this time. The records were generated to support the national Fire Program Analysis system. This project analyzes important factors such as causes, date of occurrences, top states, etc. and gathers all meaningful information to make clear and beautiful visualizations.

Data Sources: https://www.kaggle.com/rtatman/188-million-us-wildfires
